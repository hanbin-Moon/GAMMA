GAMMA

We introduce a new methodology (GAMMA) that is capable of simultaneously analyzing many phenotypes and correcting for population structure. Our paper presents this method and demonstrates that GAMMA is an improvement over other methods, which either fail to detect true effects or produce many false positive identifications.
